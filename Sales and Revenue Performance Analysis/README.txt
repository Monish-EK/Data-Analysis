Sales and Revenue Performance Analysis

This project focused on analyzing sales, customer behavior, and revenue data to uncover actionable insights for improving business operations. By examining trends in product performance, customer contributions, and city-wise revenue, the analysis identified key drivers of success and areas requiring optimization. Advanced visualizations, created through Excel dashboards, provided stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales performance and operational challenges. The insights derived were instrumental in enhancing resource allocation, streamlining processes, and addressing inefficiencies, ultimately boosting operational efficiency and revenue growth.
